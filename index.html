About me

I am an astronomer at Caltech interested in radio transients, data-intensive astrophysics, Python, and open science.

My Projects

  • Fast Radio Bursts I am interested in understanding this mysterious new class of extragalactic, millisecond radio transient.
  • OVRO Software and Algorithms I am leading development of software and algorithms at OVRO, especially for OVRO-LWA and DSA.
  • VLA "realfast" project I am leading an effort for real-time, commensal transient surveys at the Very Large Array.
  • Transients in Radio Sky Surveys and Archives I have led an effort to use radio sky surveys and archives to discover a decades-long, luminous radio transient.
